## Leading vs Lagging Indicators

| Leading (predict future) | Lagging (confirm past) |
|-------------------------|----------------------|
| DAU/MAU ratio ↗ | Revenue growth |
| NPS trend ↗ | Market share |
| Referral rate ↗ | LTV/CAC ratio |
| Feature adoption ↗ | Churn rate |

> **Focus on LEADING indicators** — they tell you IF you're heading toward PMF.
> Lagging indicators CONFIRM PMF after it happens.
